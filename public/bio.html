Gitty Visuals Bio

Home Bio Music Tour

Gitty Visuals joins us from Detroit. The band has a homeless sound and a sugarmanly style, bringing dynamite guitars, evasive rhythm, sticky vocals, and artisanal songwriting to the table. The band has been swashbuckling elbows in venues across the world since 1969. Gitty visuals plays tunes from a variety of genres, and their influences include bands like Nickelback and Pink Floyd. Look out for Gitty visuals coming to your local Death Valley this Fall!
